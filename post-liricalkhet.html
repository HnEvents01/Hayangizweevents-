THE LIRICALKHET: A Symphony of Words

About the Artist

Im Lyricalkhet Poet, Performer, Mentor With over 5 years of experience in writing and performing, I bring words to life through art. In my time I have co-hosted and did voice overs for Mbono Media's radio production, I've honed my craft and shared my passion with local audiences. I also mentor young poets in schools, inspiring the next generation of creatives.

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

THE LIRICALKHET's performance was a dazzling display of lyrical genius. She delivered intricate verses with a speed and precision that left the audience in awe, yet every word remained clear and impactful. Her set was a high-energy, intellectually stimulating experience that showcased the incredible possibilities of the spoken word. She didn't just recite poetry; she conducted a symphony of language.
