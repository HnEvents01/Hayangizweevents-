MALUNGELO & LIZWI: A Duet of Poetic Harmony

About the Artists

Malungelo is a poet, a singer songwriter, and an audiobooks story telling, she was born and raised in KZN, she’s an performing artist, A 2024 winner of ikhaya lamaciko happing every Friday on ukhozi FM .she’s also a position 2 winner of Slam poetry 2025 that was hosted by Ekuruleni municipality,She visits schools back home in KZN where she helps on teach the young ones poetry in isizulu

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

The collaborative performance by MALUNGELO & LIZWI was a moment of pure magic. Malungelo’s powerful, rhythmic verses intertwined perfectly with Lizwi's hauntingly beautiful vocal runs, creating a soundscape that was both epic and intimate. It was an innovative and breathtaking performance that transcended the boundaries of traditional spoken word. The synergy between the two artists was palpable, and they left the audience completely mesmerized.
