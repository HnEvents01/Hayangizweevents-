Event Report | Published August 20, 2025

BLACK CHILD: The Voice of a Generation

About the Artist

BLACK CHILD is a storyteller at heart, a griot for the modern age. His poetry is a powerful conduit for the narratives of his community, tackling issues of social justice, heritage, and resilience with unflinching honesty. His stage presence is magnetic, characterized by a raw, emotive energy that transforms his words into a visceral experience for the audience.

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

On the night of August 2nd, BLACK CHILD held the audience in the palm of his hand. His performance was a fiery, passionate display of spoken word at its most impactful. Each line was delivered with a conviction that resonated throughout the venue, earning him thunderous applause. He spoke truth to power, yet his message was one of hope and empowerment, solidifying his status as a vital voice in the contemporary poetry scene.
