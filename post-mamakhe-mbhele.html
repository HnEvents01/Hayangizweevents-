BILINGUAL PERFORMANCE

MAMAKHE MBHELE

Headliner Performance | Woman in Poetry Showcase
▶ Watch MAMAKHE's Performance

Mamakhe Mbhele brought a powerful maternal energy to the stage during her headlining performance at the Woman in Poetry showcase. She commanded the stage with authority, reciting her poems in both English and Sesotho, seamlessly weaving between languages to create a rich tapestry of cultural expression. Her poetry, rich with themes of heritage, motherhood, and cultural preservation, resonated deeply with an audience that spanned generations.
