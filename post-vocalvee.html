VOCALVEE: The Perfect Opening Note

About the Artist

"Vuyisile Nkosana, know as Vocalvee Rsa, born November 27, 1999, is a multifaceted musician from South Africa. Known for his versatility, Vuyisile crafts music across various genres, united by a common thread - motivation and healing. His songs are more than just melodies; they're messages of hope and inspiration. A recipient of the Best Performance certificate in Cape Town, Vuyisile continues to spread his uplifting art. Currently based in Ivory Park Extension 2, he's dedicated to sharing his gift with the world."

Opening Act at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

VOCALVEE had the honor of opening the show, and she set the bar incredibly high for the evening. From her very first note, she captivated the audience with her stunning vocals and heartfelt performance. Her set created an atmosphere of intimacy and excitement, perfectly warming up the crowd for the night of poetry that was to follow. It was a beautiful and memorable start to the event, showcasing a phenomenal talent who is undoubtedly destined for great things.
