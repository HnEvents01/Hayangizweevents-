THABANG MADUNA: The Master of Ceremonies

About the MC

Thabang Maduna The Artistic director, writer, Choreographer, Actor ,Mc, Facilitator, Performer, Storyteller Very committed to every skill that I have to work in. very passionate with kids. Artist and Leadership with more than 10 years experience of professional performance and 7years in working with kids broad range of different types of creating art movement skills with these of modern days and other in making sure to bring the best in the work I do.

Role at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

As one of the evening's hosts, Thabang was the driving force behind the incredible atmosphere. He guided the audience through the night with effortless charm and humor, introducing each poet with genuine admiration and respect. His professional yet personable approach created a warm and electric environment, making both the performers and the audience feel right at home. The success of the event's flow and energy owes a great deal to his masterful hosting.
