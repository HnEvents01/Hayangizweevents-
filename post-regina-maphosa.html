REGINA MAPHOSA: Grace and Poise on Stage

About the MC

She is Regina Maphosa a 24 years old , who is Originally from Zimbabwe but grew up in IVORY PARK.,she started doing art in 2016 doing school plays and theater .Regina is a vibrant young intelligent hustler who happens to be a poet too .she have been doing art for 9 years and have performed in many places also expanded into presenting under 4ever stream tv a show called USIZO LWAMI an MC as well and a GBV motivational speaker founder of THE ROAD TO HEALING .I am a confident hustler owing REGINA M D'HUSTELR .Regina is a neutral mood giver who sees to everyone concerns..I am brave and Smart

Role at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

Co-hosting the Big 5 Poetry Experience, Regina was the epitome of grace and professionalism. She provided insightful introductions for each artist and maintained a wonderful rapport with the audience throughout the night. Her calm and poised demeanor was the perfect complement to the high emotional energy of the performances, ensuring the evening felt both exciting and impeccably curated. Regina’s presence was a key ingredient in the event's sophisticated and welcoming tone.
