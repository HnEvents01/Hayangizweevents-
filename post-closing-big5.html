Event Highlight

Event Report | Published August 20, 2025

A Monumental Closing to an Unforgettable Night

The Final Bow

The Big 5 Poetry Experience, held on August 2, 2025, culminated in a powerful and unifying moment. After an evening filled with breathtaking performances that spanned the full spectrum of human emotion, all the featured poets, hosts, and performers gathered on stage for a final, resounding applause from a deeply moved audience.

A Celebration of Art and Community

This closing moment was more than just a curtain call; it was a celebration of the vibrant spoken word community that Haya Ngizwe Events is proud to cultivate. It represented the unity, respect, and shared passion that defined the entire event. The energy in the room was electric—a mixture of awe for the talent witnessed and gratitude for the shared experience. The night solidified the Big 5 Poetry Experience as a landmark event, a true testament to the power of voice and the magic of live performance.
