Poet Spotlight

Event Report | Published August 20, 2025

DARK DINDI: Elegance and Emotional Depth

About the Artist

Thabiso Mariri is praise singer and an actress. She was born and raised in Ivory park Midrand. She did poetry and acting since Sunday school. Inspired by Inqanawe Yangempela and Mr Enoch Shishenge. Debuted in 2023 in her late high school years. She won 1st position in Segarona cultural Festival competition in both district and provincial level informal poetry in 2023. She became a defending champion in the same competition in 2024. She was awarded as cultural learner of the year 2023/24. She won 1st place in the KAE fun fest competition 2023. Became one of the special guest poets to Ntombhi Ya Mutsonga’s very first multilingual poetry festival in 2023. In 2024 she became an RCL in the name of promoting Sports and culture in her school. She is also an Aesthetic content creator and the founder of Voices of the Silenced.

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

In a night of powerful voices, DARK DINDI's performance was a moment of profound stillness and beauty. She commanded the stage not with volume, but with the sheer weight of her words and the honesty of her emotion. The audience was hushed, hanging on every carefully chosen phrase. Her set was an elegant and deeply moving display of poetic artistry, proving that true power often lies in subtlety and grace.
