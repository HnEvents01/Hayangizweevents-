PHOKA DE POET: The Rhythmic Storyteller

About the Artist

My name is Keorapetse Motloung, Also known as Phoka De Poet a passionate actor, storyteller, and poet with a voice that echoes the hearts of the voiceless. Since grade three, love for poetry has been a burning flame, fueling his creativity and drive. Born and bred in Qwaqwa Free state, he later pursued his dreams in Gauteng, where he honed his craft and made a name for himself in the arts scene. Guided by mentorship of Vusi Nkwenkwezi, an award-winning actor and theatre practitioner, Phoka discovered the power of acting and its ability to deliver impactful messages. With a flair for storytelling, he weaves words into vivid way that transport audiences to new worlds. Phoka's journey has been marked by notable milestones, including appearances in local newspapers like Qwaqwa Newspaper (2015) and Kempton Express (2023). His TV debut on Soweto TV (2025) was a testament to his growing influence and artistry. As a poet, Phoka's words are a reflection of his soul - raw, honest, and unapologetic. He's collaborated with fellow poets, pushing boundaries and exploring new ways to express the human experience. With numerous awards under his belt, he's motivated to continue creating, inspiring, and empowering others through his art. Phoka is ready to take his craft to new heights. Stay tuned for more from this talented artist, who's redefining the boundaries of poetry and storytelling.

PERFORMANCE AT THE BIG 5 POETRY EXPERIENCE:

PHOKA DE POET brought an undeniable spiritual and smooth energy to the Big 5 stage. His set was a masterclass in performance dynamics, seamlessly shifting between gentle, melodic verses and powerful, impactful crescendos. The audience was visibly moved, swaying to the rhythm of his words. His ability to connect on such a personal level while maintaining a commanding stage presence was a true highlight of the event.
