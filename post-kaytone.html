KAYTONE: The Soulful Truth-Teller

About the Artist

Kgaugelo Moeng commonly known as Kaytone is born and raised in Ivory Park under the artistic eye of Josaya Langa, Kenneth "Beast" Lukhele and currently growing with Nash under Cadre's Production. She first fell in love with writing back in 2016/17 during her high school days. She is an executive member , writer and Performer at Sanibonani Creatives. She has had the priviledge to be a part of Countless competitions and festivals such as Tembisa Theatre week, ishashalazi theatre festival, Ekurhuleni poetry slam just to highlight some. She had the opportunity to share the stage and work with the likes of Mmabatho Chuene, Ashley Moyo, Themba Masilela , Sir Nash L. Makgobane just to name a few, she has performed at south African state theatre, TX theatre, KCAP in KZN and other art centres. she has worked with other community arts development groups such Intokozo theatre company , and PEU arts development. Over the years she has gained experience in poetry writing and performing, script writing, assistant directing, project management, events management and Stage management all in the space of theatres and poetry. She believes that you cannot learn to swim by studying the water you ought to get in and learn as you drown ❤️

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

KAYTONE delivered one of the most soulful and resonant performances of the evening. Her stage presence was warm and inviting, yet her words carried an undeniable fire. She spoke on issues of identity and justice with a clarity and passion that was both enlightening and inspiring. The audience responded with snaps, cheers, and a standing ovation, moved by the truth and soul she poured into her art.
