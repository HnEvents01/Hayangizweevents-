YOUNGER DRILL: The Modern Wordsmith

About the Artist

*Ayabulela Jantjies (Younger Drill)* Acclaimed poet and wordsmith, Ayabulela Jantjies, known by his stage name Younger Drill (Mr talking to my self), has been weaving magic with words since 2018. With a passion for storytelling and self-expression, Younger Drill's poetry explores themes of identity, love, and social commentary, resonating with audiences and inspiring a new generation of poetry. = Born with a gift for lyrical expression, Younger Drill's journey as a poet began in 2018. Through his work, he aims to spark meaningful conversations, challenge perspectives, and celebrate the human experience. His unique voice and style have earned him recognition as a talented and innovative poet. *Younger Drill's Style* Younger Drill's poetry is characterized by its raw emotion, vivid imagery, and unapologetic honesty. His words are a reflection of his own experiences, observations, and insights, crafted to inspire, provoke, and uplift. *Experience* - Active in the poetry scene since 2018 - Performed at various events and platforms. - Collaborated with fellow artists and writers.

Performance at The Big 5 Poetry Experience

YOUNGER DRILL lit up the stage with a vibrant and energetic performance. His delivery was sharp, fast-paced, and full of the unapologetic confidence of his generation. He connected instantly with the younger members of the audience while earning the respect of all with his clever lyricism and poignant social commentary. His set was a burst of modern energy that proved the future of poetry is in very capable hands.
